Ramakrishnan Hareesh

MUSIC COMPOSER | PERFORMER | MULTI - INSTRUMENTALIST
Crafting music for the soul

I am Ramakrishnan Hareesh, a music composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist from Kerala, India. With six years of experience in the music industry, I create music across various genres, especially indie music. I have had the privilege of collaborating with renowned artists like Vidhu Prathap and have been honored with the Indywood Award for Best Music Producer in India and the UAE. I also perform live, including at two TEDx programs.

My music is a reflection of my passion and creativity. I aim to create music that resonates with people and makes a lasting impact in their hearts.
